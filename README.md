Dorm Connect

Dorm Connect is a modern mobile application designed to streamline and digitize dormitory management for educational institutions. The platform provides an integrated system for students and dorm administrators to manage accommodation, report issues, communicate updates, and maintain efficient dorm operations.

The goal of Dorm Connect is to replace manual dormitory management processes with a centralized digital platform that improves communication, transparency, and operational efficiency within student housing environments.

⸻

Key Features

Student Features
	•	Secure Authentication – Students can securely register and log in to access dorm services.
	•	Room Information Access – View dormitory and room allocation details.
	•	Maintenance Request System – Submit maintenance or facility repair requests.
	•	Issue & Complaint Reporting – Report dorm-related problems directly to administrators.
	•	Announcements & Notifications – Receive important updates from dorm management.

Administrator Features
	•	Resident Management – Manage student registrations and dorm allocations.
	•	Maintenance Tracking – Monitor and resolve maintenance requests efficiently.
	•	Communication System – Send announcements and updates to dorm residents.
	•	Operational Monitoring – Track complaints and dorm facility issues.

⸻

Problem Statement

Dormitory management in many institutions still relies on manual processes, spreadsheets, and fragmented communication channels. This leads to:
	•	Delayed maintenance responses
	•	Inefficient communication between residents and administrators
	•	Poor tracking of issues and requests

Dorm Connect addresses these challenges by providing a centralized digital platform for managing dormitory operations effectively.

⸻

Solution

Dorm Connect provides a mobile-first solution that enables seamless interaction between students and dorm administrators. The system centralizes dormitory services including issue reporting, communication, and room management within a single application.

By digitizing these processes, Dorm Connect improves response times, enhances transparency, and simplifies dormitory administration.

⸻

System Architecture

Dorm Connect follows a client–server architecture:
	1.	Mobile Client – Provides the user interface for students and administrators.
	2.	Backend Server – Handles business logic, authentication, and request processing.
	3.	Database Layer – Stores information such as students, rooms, complaints, and maintenance requests.

This architecture ensures scalability, maintainability, and efficient data management.
